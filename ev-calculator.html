Understanding Expected Value

EV Formula
EV = (Win Probability × Profit If Win) − (Loss Probability × Stake)
A positive EV means the bet is profitable in the long run, even if you lose on any single bet.
Kelly Criterion
The optimal fraction of your bankroll to bet is: f* = (bp − q) / b, where b = decimal odds − 1, p = win prob, q = 1 − p. This maximises long-run growth while avoiding ruin.
ROI vs EV
EV% (ROI per bet) tells you edge per dollar wagered. A 5% ROI at $100/bet over 1,000 bets projects to $5,000 profit — but variance means real results will deviate from expected values, especially over small samples.

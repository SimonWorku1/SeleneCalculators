About Devig Methods

Multiplicative (Standard)
Scales each implied probability by the same factor so they sum to 100%. Most common and assumes the book distributes vig proportionally across all outcomes.
Additive
Subtracts an equal share of overround from each implied probability. Best for markets where you expect the book to add flat vig across outcomes.
Power / Shin
Uses a power model (Shin's method) to solve for the margin analytically. Generally considered the most accurate for heavily-vig'd markets.
